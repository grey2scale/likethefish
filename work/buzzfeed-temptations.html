BuzzFeed + Temptations

Role: Creative Director

We partnered with renowned cat snack specialists Temptations to promote visibility for the Snacky Mouse — a tiny, adorable contraption filled to the brim with mouthwatering cat treats. The Creative Development team worked closely with BuzzFeed's internal Games team to create Snacky Mouse Rumble, a lighthearted beat-em-up that put the Snacky Mouse front-and-center in an addictive, fast-paced gaming environment.
