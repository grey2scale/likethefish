Millennial Swipe Simulator

Role: Design, Programming, Art — everything

Swipe right! Swipe left! No matter what you do, just keep swiping! A kinda cynical little mini-game I made back in 2015 to learn about iOS development. Since removed from the App Store at the request of Match.com for using the word 'Swipe' in its title. It's 2015! Welcome to the thrilling world of app-based dating. See if you have what it takes to find true love like a true millennial — swipe for more matches, more matches means more points, more points means less boredom, less boredom means more swiping. Whatever you do, don't stop swiping.
