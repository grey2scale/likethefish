Death to Cupid

Role: Art & Animation

I handled the art and animation for Death to Cupid, a browser-based, gory-as-heck anti-Valentine's Day game for the rad folks at DamnJoan.

Game design and development by Joe Laquinte. Music composition by Danny Ryan at James Warburton Music.
