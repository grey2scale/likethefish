Death to Cupid

Art & Animation

I handled the art and animation for Death to Cupid, a browser-based, gory-as-heck anti-Valentine's Day game for the rad folks at DamnJoan. The game features fully original character illustrations inspired by classical Cupid imagery — with a decidedly irreverent twist.
