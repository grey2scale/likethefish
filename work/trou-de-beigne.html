Trou de Beigne: Donut Dash!!

Design, Programming, Art — everything

In 2018 I developed a free mobile game to promote the wonderful eats at Montreal's own Trou de Beigne. Donut Dash!! is an iPhone and Android game where you play as one of Montreal's trademark snowy owls, dashing, diving, and swooping across the city's skyline to stop an avian donut heist. The game comes fully-featured with endless waves of enemies, steadily increasing difficulty, unlockable power-ups, and a living leaderboard with a chance to win a free donut at the real Trou de Beigne at the end of every month!
