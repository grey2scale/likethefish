My Garbage Cat Wakes Me Up At 3AM Every Day

Role: Design, Programming, Art — everything

My Garbage Cat Wakes Me Up At 3AM Every Day is a short, quirky browser-based cat chaos simulator I made in Construct 2. You play as my garbage cat, who wakes me up at 3AM literally every morning. Knock everything over until your owner wakes up. Use the arrow keys to move and jump. Press Z to meow and cry all the time. Press X to knead with your dumb little paws. Do your worst.
