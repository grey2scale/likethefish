Writing 2010

GamePro: Alice: Madness Returns

Writer, Journalist

Fulfilling my wildest cyberpunk fantasies, I trekked to Shanghai in the midst of the World Expo 2010 to sit down with influential game designer — and self-proclaimed 'wicked Walt Disney' — American McGee to discuss his ex-pat status, the scrappy studio he'd sprouted on the outskirts of China's metropolis, and the follow up to his most well-known work: Alice: Madness Returns. Cover story written for GamePro Magazine, September 2010 issue.
