Smash n' Dab

Design, Programming, Art, Motion Capture — everything

Dad's gotta impress a squad of rowdy teens with his sick dance moves and dabs so 'lit' you'll have to wear shades. Smash n' Dab is a browser-based arcade game where you have to dab into, and destroy, as many items thrown your way as possible. Just don't dab on the cat. Commissioned by the super rad folks at [adult swim] and created in Unity. Art, design, programming, motion capture/dance moves, etc, by me. Music/sfx courtesy Audiojungle and freesound.org. Featured in the Indie Showcase at the 2023 Smithsonian Arcade.
