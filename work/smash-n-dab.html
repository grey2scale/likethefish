Smash n' Dab

Role: Design, Programming, Art, Motion Capture — everything

Dad's gotta impress a squad of rowdy teens with his sick dance moves and dabs so "lit" you'll have to wear shades B)

Smash n' Dab is a browser-based arcade game where you have to ~dab~ into, and destroy, as many items thrown your way as possible. Just don't dab on the cat. Commissioned by the super rad folks at [adult swim] and built in Unity. Art, design, programming, motion capture/dance moves — all me. Music and SFX courtesy Audiojungle and freesound.org.

Featured in the Indie Showcase at the 2023 Smithsonian American Art Museum Arcade.
