GamePro: The Last Guardian

Writer, Journalist

I travelled to Tokyo in the final weeks of winter 2011 to sit down with legendary — and legendarily reclusive — video game designer Fumito Ueda. We discussed adolescence, art, interactivity, and how it all intersects in his long-gestating magnum opus, The Last Guardian. Cover story written for GamePro Magazine, May 2011 issue.
