Brands Motion 2018–2019

Twitch

Branded Content Strategist, Custom Solutions

As a Branded Content Strategist on Twitch's fantastic Custom Solutions team, I wore a lot of creative hats. I served as one of the primary points of contact for creative ideation, branded content strategy, and client-facing design work — producing pitch decks, campaign concepts, copy, illustrations, branded emotes, and broadcast and experiential execution mock-ups. I also created original animation and motion design for larger-than-life branded livestream executions — producing animated overlays and custom emotes for high-profile branded broadcasts, and integrating cutting-edge character animation software to push Twitch's branded livestreams into new interactive territory. I pitched and helped execute off-the-wall experiential activations, including a pedicab-inspired TwitchCon stunt that turned Totino's 'Couch Hard' brand campaign into a live event. I concepted, designed, and animated custom media spots for brand partners featuring top influencer talent, including a series of 8-bit-inspired spots for Progressive starring OMGItsFirefoxx.
