BuzzFeed: Creative Animation

Role: Director of Creative Development

It was a sincere passion of mine to introduce more original, high-quality artwork to BuzzFeed Creative's branded offerings — which led to the formation of the Creative Animation team.

As Director of Creative Development, I founded and led the team: highly talented animators, designers, and illustrators specializing in original artwork to tell brands' stories on BuzzFeed and across the social web. The team produced work for Microsoft, IBM, Coca-Cola, Kohl's, Intel, GE, AT&T, Gap, and Spotify, among others.
