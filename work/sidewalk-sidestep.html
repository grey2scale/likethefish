Sidewalk Sidestep

Role: Design, Programming, Art — everything

Sidewalk Sidestep is a browser game about awkward sidewalk interactions, profuse apologies, and "Hey, I'm walkin' here!" jokes — three things that I know a lot about.

Commissioned by the super rad folks at [adult swim] and built in Unity. Art, design, programming, etc, all by me. Super catchy 8-Bit Bossa Nova by the super talented Joth (CC0).
