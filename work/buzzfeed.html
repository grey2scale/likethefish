Brands 2012–2016

BuzzFeed

Role: Senior Creative → ACD → Creative Director → Director of Creative Development

I spent four wildly formative years on BuzzFeed's Creative Advertising team, collaborating with some of the smartest and most creatively distinct people working on the Internet.

As a Senior Creative, I brainstormed and executed countless pieces of bespoke branded content for many of the world's leading brands. Later, as an Associate Creative Director — and eventually Creative Director — I partnered with BuzzFeed's sales, strategy, and product teams to develop scalable, high-value custom integrations for global clients.

Along the way, I built and managed a team of tremendously talented animators, illustrators, and designers who helped expand and diversify BuzzFeed's branded content offerings.

As Director of Creative Development, I led a team of ACDs focused on developing new ad products, bespoke branded post formats, and highly interactive integrations designed to expand BuzzFeed's native advertising platform for its largest brand partners.
