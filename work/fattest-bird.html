The Fattest Bird in Brooklyn

Design, Programming, Art — everything

My first game made in Unity, The Fattest Bird in Brooklyn is an addictive tap-and-flap arcade game where players, swooping through the brownstone-lined streets of Brooklyn, must snatch up every scrap of street bread they see in hopes of becoming the plumpest pigeon around. The game is available on the Google Play Store, and was a nominee at the inaugural Playcrafting Awards. Tap, flap, and swoop your way across the New York City skyline — gobble up all the savory street bread in your path, but watch out for bikers, hipsters, taxis, tourists, and other big city hazards along the way.
