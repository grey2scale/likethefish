BuzzFeed
Brands 2014

BuzzFeed + Android

Role: Creative Director

Tasked with creating a brand-new way for BuzzFeed users to find their next favorite post, our multi-talented team of Creative Directors conceived the Post-O-Matic: an innovative interactive that blended existing editorial content with entirely custom creative to make discovery feel fresh, fun, and genuinely useful.

The Android brand lived in the experience naturally — present but never overbearing, exactly the right tone for the platform.
